PythiaPlotter documentation
===========================

`PythiaPlotter <https://github.com/raggleton/PythiaPlotter/>`__ plots diagrams of particle decay trees from HEP Monte Carlo (MC) events. It comes in handy to figure out what is actually going on in your MC!
